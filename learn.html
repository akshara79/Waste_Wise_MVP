Quick Reference

Waste categories at a glance

Use this guide to understand examples and safe handling for each waste type.

Wet Waste

Compost when possible and avoid mixing with plastics.

Examples
  • Fruit peels
  • Leftover food
  • Tea leaves

Dry Waste

Keep recyclables clean and dry before disposal.

Examples
  • Paper and cardboard
  • Clean plastic packaging
  • Metal cans

Hazardous Waste

Store safely in original containers and use approved collection points.

Examples
  • Paint thinner
  • Pesticides
  • Chemical cleaners

E-Waste

Use certified e-waste centers and remove personal data first.

Examples
  • Old phone chargers
  • Batteries and cables
  • Broken earphones

Medical Waste

Seal items and hand over to pharmacy or hospital take-back systems.

Examples
  • Expired medicines
  • Used bandages
  • Syringes in puncture-proof containers
